Privacy Policy for Puzzle Number

Innoppia respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use our mobile game application, Puzzle Number

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personal information or sensitive data from users of Puzzle Number. The App does not require you to create an account or sign in, and it does not ask for any personal details such as your name, email address, or location.

No Personal Information Collection:

  • No Sign Up/Login: The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.
  • No Data Storage: We do not store any user data or gameplay information in a database.

2. Advertisements

Puzzle Number integrates advertisements to help support the development of the game. The advertisements may come from third-party providers, such as Google AdMob.

Third-Party Advertising:

  • AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display banner and interstitial ads in the App. AdMob may collect and use non-personal data, such as your device type, IP address, advertising identifier, and other similar information to serve relevant ads.
  • Data Collected by Ad Networks: AdMob and other third-party ad networks may collect anonymous data such as your device type, ad interactions, and location for advertising purposes. This data is not tied to your personal identity.

For more information on how AdMob collects and uses data, please refer to their Privacy Policy.

3. Analytics

We do not use any analytics services or collect any data related to gameplay, user behavior, or app usage. However, third-party ad services may track interactions with ads, which are anonymous.

4. Security

We do not store or process any sensitive user information. Since we do not collect personal data, we do not use encryption or security measures for personal data storage. However, any data collected by third-party services, such as advertising networks, is handled by those services according to their own security protocols.

5. Children's Privacy

Puzzle Number is not directed towards children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under the age of 13, we will take immediate steps to delete that information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this document with the updated effective date. We encourage you to periodically review this page for any updates.
