How to Delete Your Account

Step-by-Step Guide

If you decide to stop using the app and want to delete your account, follow the steps below. Our app uses a tab-based navigation interface to help you move between screens.

  1. Go to the "Profile" Tab: Tap on the Profile tab in the app's bottom (or top) navigation bar.
  2. Scroll Down: On the Profile screen, scroll down to the bottom of the page.
  3. Tap the "Delete" Button: You will see a red button labeled "Delete". Tap it to initiate account deletion.
  4. Confirm Deletion: A confirmation prompt will appear to ensure you really want to delete your account. Confirm to proceed.
  5. Account Removed: Once confirmed, your account and all related data will be permanently deleted.
  6. Redirected to Login: After successful deletion, the app will take you back to the Login screen.

This process ensures your data privacy and gives you full control over your account. If you change your mind, you can cancel the deletion before confirming.
