How Account Deletion Works in Our App

Our application consists of four main pages, including the Profile page. If a user decides they no longer want to use the app and wishes to delete their account, they can follow this simple process:

  1. Go to the Profile Page: From the app menu, navigate to the Profile page.
  2. Scroll to the bottom: Locate the "Delete Account" button.
  3. Confirm deletion: A confirmation message will appear to avoid accidental deletions.
  4. Account deletion: If confirmed, your account will be permanently deleted.
  5. Redirect to login: After deletion, you’ll be redirected to the Login page.

This ensures privacy, control, and a smooth user experience while handling account removal.
